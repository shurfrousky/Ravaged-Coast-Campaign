When the Gnaw waxed strong, many mortals began a mad dash to what was presumed to be the safety of the coast. It availed them less than nothing. 
as the bells of the Skaven screamed out doom, sleek cutters and imposing galleons attempting to make good their escape either were dahed to ruin and sunk,
 smashed into each other or ran aground as the waters churned. Those who staggered free of the boiling waves fell on one another in a mindless and rabid frenzy
as the unleashed magic weapons warped them into  rat-like mutants.
